freekipedia

freekipedia is an open platform to share information on a life without or with less money. Based on the assumption that the most important and fulfilling things in life are free anyway, we want to collect knowledge on how to cope several aspects of life without money, but with more freedom and pleasure. Another world is possible. The dream to be independent from money is what combines us. If this dream is also yours, join us, no matter if you're an anti-capitalist, consumption-critic, environmentalist or a tightwad, if you're poor, religiously motivated or just looking for fun. Or all at once.

contribute

freekipedia lives from its users' contribution. Everyone can write and edit articles. There are plenty ways to contribute to this project. Find out, how.

inspiration

There are plenty of examples of people and communities, who tried (and some even managed) to live without money. Find out about them.
