Contribution

Jump to: navigation, search

As freekipedia is designed as an open platform, it lives from its users' contribution. There's no editorial staff and, obviously, no get paid. Everything that is happening here come from the community, that is from you. There are several ways to contribute on this encyclopaedia on money free living. You can

  1. write new articles. You are an expert on hitchhiking, you know how to grow your own food or you have experience on a part of money free living, we never even dreamt of? Fine, share your knowledge to the community.
  2. extent new articles. There's already an article on something you know more about? That's cool. Edit it and write down all you know.
  3. fix mistakes. Whenever you stumble across a mistake, please fix it. Spelling of words, grammar, not working links, anything.
  4. design and format it. Organise articles, group subtopics (and write headlines), make that freekipedia looks good.
  5. donate webspace. You have some webspace on a server, which is currently unused. Why not donating it to freekipedia. Please leave a note in the "discussion" section of this side.

For instruction how to edit and write articles, click help.
